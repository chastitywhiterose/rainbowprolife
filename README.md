# Rainbow Pro-Life Alliance

A collection of historical importance for the Rainbow Pro-Life Alliance. It is an anti-abortion group I am a member of, and I volunteer my skills as a writer.

# Purpose

I am Chastity White Rose, the author of many books on various subjects, and also computer programs, on which I tend to write technical books. I believe in the power of words and numbers. Writing information down so that it is not lost is one of the most important things to me. For this reason, I have started to collect writings from LGBTQIA+ individuals like myself, especially those who work with the Rainbow Pro-Life Alliance now and in the past.

<https://www.rainbowprolife.org/>

There is no Pro-Life group quite like the Rainbow Pro-Life Alliance. Formerly we were called the Pro-Life Alliance of Gays and Lesbians (PLAGAL). This is where people go who wish to fight against the crime of baby murder but are not welcome because they are Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer, Intersex, Asexual or Plus (anything other label that people tend to exclude and discriminate against).
